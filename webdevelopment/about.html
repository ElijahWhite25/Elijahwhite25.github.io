Professional Background

Who is Elijah White? I’ve always considered myself a hands-on worker who stays calm when things get busy. My background in high-volume environments—from the fast-paced front counter at KFC to the logistics-heavy world of Online Grocery Pickup at Walmart—has taught me how to multitask without losing sight of the customer’s experience. I’ve learned that whether you’re managing transactions or coordinating complex orders, clear communication is what keeps a team moving.
